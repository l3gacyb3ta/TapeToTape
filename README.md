# Tape To Tape

Tape To Tape is a YSWS program where *you* make us a song, and *we* buy you an album.

We'll also compile all submission and put them up on bandcamp!

## How it works
Record your screen using Lapse, and create a song! If you don't have any experience with Sonic Pi, don't worry, there's a tutorial, follow it, and make something your own.

Then, record yourself performing it. Make it something you're proud of having made, it doesn't have to be *good* by any measure, there's no right way to prove any given song is good ;)

Submit your work!

## How you earn your album
- 1 hours spent on your song: Anything ~$5 on bandcamp
- 2 hours: Anything ~$12 on bandcamp

Then, you'll send you your song/album on bandcamp!
